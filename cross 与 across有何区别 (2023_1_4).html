cross 与 across有何区别

 

两者都可表示“穿过”，但 cross 是动词，across 是介词。比较：

他用 5 分钟就(游)过了河。

误：He swam cross the river in five minutes.

误：He acrossed the river in five minutes.

正：He crossed the river in five minutes.

正：He swam across the river in five minutes.
